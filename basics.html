Graphology Basics


Handwriting analysis deals with different areas of your life. The handwriting is a reflection of your body and your mind. Here are a few basic concepts of handwriting analysis.


Human Body and Handwriting Connection

Upper Zone


It depicts the brain (thinking part of your body): It represents our intelligence and our creative ideas.

The letters L, H, B, D, F, T represent the upper zone.

Having a well formed middle zone shows confidence and good communication skills.

A distortion in the formations of these alphabets shows a problem in the digestion, chest pain, throat pain, etc.

Middle Zone


It depicts the middle portion of our body (neck, chest, stomach): It represents our confidence and our communication skills.

The letters a, o, u, m, n, etc represent the middle zone.

Having a well formed middle zone shows confidence and good communication skills.

A distortion in the formations of these alphabets shows a problem in the digestion, chest pain, throat pain, etc.

Lower Zone


It depicts the lower portion of the body (legs and sexual organs): It represents our materialistic needs and finances.

The letters y,g, j, z represent the middle zone.

A well-formed lower zone shows financial progress.

A distortion in the formations of these alphabets shows a problem in the knee or any part of the leg.


This is how the handwriting has a connection to the body system. Through this we can identify the health problems and suggest changes.

So we are able to identify behavior as well as health problems through the handwriting.
