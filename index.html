what is Graphology?




Graphology is the study of handwriting to understand personality and behavior traits in an individual. Like a DNA sample no two handwriting samples are same there are marked differences. Just like an expert scientist a graphologist can analyze a handwriting sample and identify personality traits, attitude, mental acuity, etc. The technique involves using the physical characteristics and patterns of writing which are unique to every individual sample. Graphology is not just a diagnostic tool it is also widely used to enhance certain favorable traits and to rectify unfavorable components of a personality.

Research has proved that 90 percent of our personality traits that affect our relationships, career, social life, confidence are governed by the subconscious mind. And it's in this same powerful subconscious mind our handwriting emerges from. As we learn to hold a pencil, crayon or a pen in early childhood the grip of our fingers, the muscle tension, the pressure, etc is all controlled by our subconscious mind. Hence a handwriting sample becomes a representation of our subconscious mind on paper, which in turn makes it a great tool of analysis.
