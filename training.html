Training


Graphology can be learned by any individual. It does not restrict you to any particular field. Anyone can learn it and become a Professional Graphologist. Knowing about the theory the course is designed in a way where you not just learn the theory but practice it simultaneously. The following are the course details.




The Basic Level

   Contents
   Margins    Baselines
  Slants   Pressure
  Zones   Pastocity
  Connections   Handwriting Size
  Individual Letters   Signature Analysis

   After completing the basic level you are able to understand a complete personality of an individual (personal and professional).

   You get a certificate after successfully completing the course.

  Duration: 9 Sessions (Two and Half Hours Each)

  Fees: 10,000/-


The Advance Level

  Contents
  Diagnose Physical Illnesses   Diagnose Mental Illness
  Drawing Analysis   Spirituality and Handwriting
  The Seven Chakra System   Graphotherapy
  Alternative Therapies.

   You get a certificate after successfully completing the course.

Workshops

The Workshops are generally conducted for awareness about the subject.

  Topics For Workshops
Parenting:

The workshop talk about the importance of correct parenting and how to detect flaws in your kid’s handwriting at an early age. It gives an insight into the child’s mind and the areas where the parents need to work.
Signature Analysis:

This workshop teaches the importance of signature. The meaning of it. How it affects your personal and professional life. How to correct it.

Career Guidance:

The workshop deals with understanding the strengths and weaknesses of individuals and helping them understand the possible career options.

Awareness:

This workshop talks about the meaning and the benefits of Graphology.

School Training:

This includes training teachers into handwriting analysis.

These workshops are conducted on requests of individuals and groups.

Duration: It can be scheduled as per the requirements.
