Training


Graphology can be learned by any individual. It does not restrict you to any particular field. Anyone can learn it and become a Professional Graphologist. Knowing about the theory the course is designed in a way where you not just learn the theory but practice it simultaneously. The following are the course details.



The Basic Level

Contents:

  • Margins
  • Baselines
  • Slants
  • Pressure
  • Zones
  • Pastocity
  • Connections
  • Handwriting Size
  • individual Letters
  • Signature Analysis

After completing the basic level you are able to understand a complete personality of an individual (personal and professional).

You get a certificate after successfully completing the course.

Duration: 9 sessions of two and a half hours each.

Fees: 10,000/- Rupees.
