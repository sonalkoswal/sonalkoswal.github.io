About





Sonal has been practicing Graphology from the past six years. She has been designing therapies for people suffering from psychological disorders helping them to change their lives. She's been providing Graphotherapy to individuals to help them deal with health issues like cardiovascular problems, arthritis, back pains, conception related issues, etc. Sonal has also helped people with life issues like finance, career, education, relationship problems, stage fear, etc.

Sonal is also a Reiki Healer, a technique which uses meditation and vedic mantras to heal people and help them to deal with problems. Her courses and counseling have a unique combination of Graphology, Graphotherapy and Meditation techniques.

She has also conducted workshops and training sessions in schools, colleges and Rotary Club. She has been invited to give a lecture on career guidance and motivation in different colleges.

In the past six years she has been successful in teaching and helping people in various parts of the world.

She has students and clients in India, Singapore, Australia, Nigeria, Sri Lanka and other parts of the world.
