About





Sonal has been practicing Graphology from the past six years. She has been designing therapies for people suffering from addiction and has helped them to come out of it in a very short period of time. She is associated with Santulan NGO which is the only NGO in India that provides free treatment. She is also associated with Practical Life Skills Farm (Mental Health Center) and here she designs therapies for people suffering from psychological disorders helping them to change their lives. She's been providing Graphotherapy to individuals to help them deal with health issues like cardiovascular problems, arthritis, back pains, conception related issues, etc. Sonal has also helped people with life issues like finance, career, education, relationship problems, stage fear, etc.

Sonal is also a Reiki Healer, a technique which uses meditation and vedic mantras to heal people and help them to deal with problems. Her courses and counseling have a unique combination of Graphology , Graphotherapy and Meditation techniques.

She has also conducted workshops and training sessions in schools, colleges and Rotary Club. She has been invited to give a lecture on career guidance and motivation in different colleges.
